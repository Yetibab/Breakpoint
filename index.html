Breakpoint

A luxury golf course in the heart of North Carolina

Welcome

At Breakpoint, golf is more than just a game—it’s a way to bring people together. Tucked among the pines of Pinehurst, our course is designed for players of all ages and skill levels to enjoy the challenge, the outdoors, and the friendships that come with every round.

Our Course

Breakpoint offers 18 holes that are fun, fair, and full of variety. Wide fairways, natural sand features, and scenic views create an experience that keeps golfers coming back, whether you’re playing your first round or your hundredth. Each hole is built to be memorable—and best enjoyed with good company.
